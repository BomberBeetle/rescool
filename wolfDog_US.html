Street dog confused with wild wolf is saved

Quando a the Hope For Paws charity received a call saying that there was a wolf roaming about a Los Angeles neighbourhood, they were suspicious, because wolves don't normally end up wandering into the city. Gladly, quando o when rescue arrived, the "wolf" had walked inside the yard of a house with a gate, which was quickly closed.

Upon being given a closer inspection, the "wolf" actually was shown to be a dog-wolf hybrid, and actually a girl. With some food and interaction, the dog calmed down and could be transported. She was in pain and with open wounds, and was treated and alocated to a foster home.
