How to a Adopt a Pet

Step 1 - Preparations

When adopting a pet, make sure you can afford to care for the animal. Certain pets, such as dogs, require a lot of attention and economical resources to care for them. Make sure your house has enough space for the animal, as well that you can reserve at least 2 hours a day to care for your friend. Research the type of animal you want to adopt - what it eats, special care and other individualities.

Step 2 - Find a shelter

Now, it's time to decide from where your new friend will come from: if you are in the US, you can use this link to find an animal shelter near you. You can also use any of the links on the bar on the side of your screen. After you find a shelter near you, make sure it is reputable - the ones from the links here are all safe, but if you get yours from somewhere else, be sure to verify it. If wanted, we can verify the place for you: send an E-Mail to verify@rescool.net or contact us here.

Step 3 - Meet your pet

When choosing an animal to take home be aware of it's conditions: every pet is different, and some will require more attention because of trauma or handicaps. Some shelters will require you to show them your living space and aptitude to take care of the animal - when questioned, answer truthfully.

Final Notes

While almost everyone wants to adopt younger, healthier animals, i encourage you to adopt the ones that older or have health problems or trauma. You could be the difference in their lives ;)
