Puppies stuck in tar are saved

In Undaipur, Índia, where the Animal Aid Unlimited charity operates, concerned residents found 3 babies engulfed in tar.They looked like they were trapped for hours, without food nor water. One of the puppies even had his mouth stuck open! The tar was stuck to the rocks, making their rescue harder.

After the tar was separated from the rocks, it was still stuck to the puppies. Hours of voluntary work with dishsoap and oil was required to remove all the tar from the puppies, but in the end they were fine.
