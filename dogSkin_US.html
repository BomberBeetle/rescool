Dog in critical state saved in India

The indian organization Animal Aid Unlimited found him in the side of a road, almost indistiguishible from a pile of rocks. He didn't accept human interaction and was extremely spooked.



While resisting to some cuddles, the people from the shelter offered all the necessary cares, he started to recover. After 2 months of intensive treatment, his skin had recovered and he was much better. Luckily, he doesn't even look like the sad little shell of a dog he was!
