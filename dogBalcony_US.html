Dog stuck in balcony in Colombia is saved

The dog, called Luna, was saved by a man called Diego Andrés Dávila Jimenez, who was on his apartment on Bogotá, when he received a alarming call about Luna, who belongs to his neighbour, that she had slipped off the balcony. She was hanging by her hind legs and hips.

Reportedly there was a crowd on the floor, whatching the situation in awe. Jimenez first tried to help the dog climb back into safety with a broomstick from the apartment below, which didn't work. So, without hesitation, he ended up making a risky rescue, having to scale the building.
