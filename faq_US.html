FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

P: Where are the stories taken from?

R: On every story, you can find their sources in the end of the page, on the form of a link, in green. On the stories' page, click or touch the link to go to the source.

P: Does the site make any income?

The site does not get any type of income, not having any type of ad for income and being manteined exclusively by free services.

P: What is the watermark in the bottom of the page?

The watermark on the bottom of the page exists because of the site's web provider, which is free. Because of the gratuity, the site has to maintain the provider's watermark.

P: What does the site's name mean?

The site's name is a combination of the words Rescue and Cool. The intention behind the name is to be easily recognized as a site about rescue, but to also give a "younger" air to the site.

P: What is the site's purpose?

Beyond simply bringing animal rescue stories, the site tries to raise awareness of mistreated or abused animal, and to inspired the readers to take action about those matters, just like what happened to me (the site's creator) after seeing and reading similar stories.
