Bear exploited for 19 years rescued

Masha passed most of her life on a 2 square meter cage behind of a van, where she was locked, beamor on intense heat of extreme cold. She's gone from a life of being gagged and jumping hoops for a crowd to a wide open space with 350 squares meters.

“I was sad with giving her up because i was with her every day of my life”, admitted Sasha, the bear's trainer, who had convived with the animal since she was 3 months old. “But i felt very good because i saw that they cared and built a really good space for her, i didn't expect it was going to be so big and nice." There isn't proof that Sasha has mistreated Masha, but Lionel De Lange, who was part of the rescue team, said that she put up her paws to apparently protect her head and body every time she saw a bar that was pointed at her.
