Cat stuck in well for 5 days rescued

The cat, who was named Jessie, fell in a well with 13 meters of depth on South Carolina in the 11th of May , and was rescued just in the 16th. The rescuers got help from people on the internet, a plumbing service and an animal health center, that helped in the healing of the animal.



If you liked this story, consider donating to Hope for Paws, the rescue association that made possible the rescue of Jessie.
