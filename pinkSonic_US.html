Dyed hedgehog saved by Canadian couple

Floyd was given to his rescuees in a shoebox - just one of the abuses this hedgehog must have suffered. He was probably bought in a pet shop, and the previous owners, who probably didn't know how to care for the animal, dyed his quills pink with dye for poodles, advertising him on Facebook as a "rare albino hedgehog".

Originally, the couple would care for him while he didn't find another home, but they decided to keep him so people would not just adopt him for his colors.
