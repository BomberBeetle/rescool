Horse stuck in hayloft rescued

A horse owner in Botetourt County, Virginia in the US found one of her horses, Pheonix, stuck on her barn's hayloft after a fight with another mare. The horses broke the wooden barrier that stopped them from reaching the stairs that lead up there.

The horse's owner tried to make the horse go down the stairs, but she couldn't. After looking for help on Facebook, she contacted the Little Fork Volunteer Technical Large Animal Team, that, as the name implies, specializes on rescuing large animals. She was advised keep the animal as calm as possible. After arriving in the place, the rescue team came up with a plan: The animal would be sedated and then slided down the stairs on top of a board.

But, the fight hadn't stopped yet. After being rescued from the hayloft, Pheonix went into respiratory arrest, and had to go through a emergencial tracheotomy. She is well now.
