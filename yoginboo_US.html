Two dogs found in school

These 2 dogs were found in a busy urban area with a lot of movement: The rescuers had to wait 28 hours to trap the dogs, which were named "Yogi" and "Boo-Boo". They were extremely nervous, because they never had any other contact with humans.


When they were finally captured, they were dirty and malnourished.The capture itself was extremely hard; the rescuers had to jump the school's fence, and also had to install their own portable fence. Furthermore, a net was used to capture them: it's important say it was done in the most humane way possible.
