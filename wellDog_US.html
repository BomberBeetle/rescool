Dog stuck inside well in a remote Indian village for 5 days is saved

In a remote village, kilometers away from Udaipur, India, the city where the Animal Aid Unlimited charity operates, locals heard the desperate cries of a dog coming out of a well. They wanted to get help, but couldn't for 5 days, until the charitie's phone number was passed down to them.

The city of Udaipur, for reference

However, when rescue arrived, there was a problem: it wasn't possible to simply send someone down the well with rope attached to them, so there was the necessity to call up a crane to get the dog out of there. The dog is well now.
